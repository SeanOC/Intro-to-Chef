Final Advice

  • The Opscode Wiki is your friend
  • Design and use simple, reusable, composable blocks (legos).
  • Don't hard code anything. Use attributes, roles, databags, and search
  • Need help? Join #chef on freenode. The Opscode crew is awesome and they don't bite (ususally).
